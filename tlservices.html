Our Truckload Services.

FreightCenter’s extensive network of carriers provide access to a wide range of truckload equipment types including flatbeds, enclosed trailers, refrigerated trailers, rail freight, double drop trailers, removable gooseneck (RGN) trailers, step decks, as well as stretch trailers that can haul oversized truck freight.

Flatbed

Flat, open bed trailer to ship larger items like manufacturing and construction equipment.

Dry Van

Fully enclosed 53’ long trailer. Best option when transporting normal packaged freight that has no specific instructions or special requirements.

Refrigerated

When you need to ship refrigerated, frozen or temperature-sensitive items.

Step Deck

Best when a load needs to be closer to the ground to accommodate forklift loading and unloading.

Removable Gooseneck

Lowboy trailer with detachable front and very low decks that allow freight to be driven onto trailer.
