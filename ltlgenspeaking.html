LTL Generally Speaking...

Used when cargo is between 150 and 15,000 pounds and is too big for parcel but too small for an entire freight trailer.

Used to maximize savings. With LTL, you only pay for the space you need, resulting in cost savings.

Is palletized and crated. Using pallets and crates reduces rates and lowers damage risks.

Delivered in the mornings. Pickups typically are in the afternoon.

Can have longer transit times than full truckload because of shared payload.
