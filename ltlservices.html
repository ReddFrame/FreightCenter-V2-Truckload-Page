Our LTL Specialized Services.

FreightCenter’s extensive network of carriers provide access to a wide range of truckload equipment types including flatbeds, enclosed trailers, refrigerated trailers, rail freight, double drop trailers, removable gooseneck (RGN) trailers, step decks, as well as stretch trailers that can haul oversized truck freight.

Expedited Shipping Services

For time-sensitive freight shipments, choose expedited shipping services. Service levels include 3-day, 2-day, overnight and next-day shipping.

Liftgate Services

No forklift or loading dock? No problem. Many of our carriers offer liftgate services for pick up and/or delivery upon request.

Notification Services

Don’t be left in the dark when it comes to your shipment. Notification services can be arranged before pick up or delivery, so you know when to expect the truck.

White Glove Services

Some things are just too valuable to handle normally. For that extra set of hands and extra peace of mind, white glove services can be requested for expensive or specialty items.

First and Final Mile Services

These services can include everything from inside pickup, packaging and dismantling to inside delivery and assembly.

Limited Access Locations

Shipping to or from a residential area? School? Construction site? Area 51? These locations make it difficult for trucks to enter. You’ll need to add limited access services.

Hazardous Materials

Items such as fuels, bio-chemicals, or pharmaceuticals are considered hazardous. We have specialized carriers that handle hazmat transportation.

Guaranteed Services

Pick-up and delivery times are estimates, and if you don’t do well with estimates, then consider adding Guaranteed Services, which includes both date and time-specific needs for pickup and delivery.
